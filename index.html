Bio

Melodic death/thrashers Vile Extortion were formed in June 2017 in Thessaloniki, Greece, having since shared the stage with big names within the Greek and global metal scene such as Sodom, Nightrage, Blaze Bayley, Chronosphere, Bio-Cancer, Exarsis and Amken.
In 2018 they finished runners-up at the Thessaloniki Battle Of The Bands, winning a contract with New Dream Records and releasing their debut EP "Incoming Threat" in May 2019.
In 2022 they performed alongside the legendary Sodom in Thessaloniki after a week-long voting contest and were featured at the 2022 Schoolwave Festival as the only extreme metal act of the weekend.
They are currently preparing their debut full-length LP with a more mature, fierce and refined approach, performing some of its material at their live shows.
